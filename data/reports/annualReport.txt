Annual Report 2025

Overview:
The company demonstrated strong overall growth throughout 2025, driven by expansion into new markets, improved product offerings, and increased customer acquisition.
While performance remained strong in key departments, operational inefficiencies and rising costs presented ongoing challenges.

Revenue Performance:
The company achieved consistent revenue growth across all quarters.
- Q1: Revenue increased by 12%
- Q2: Revenue increased by 8%
Overall annual growth remained positive but showed signs of slowing momentum in later quarters.

Regional Performance:
- APAC: Strongest performing region with high demand and rapid expansion
- Europe: Stable growth with steady customer base
- North America: Moderate growth with increasing competition

Department Performance:
- Engineering: Consistently high performance, delivering scalable and reliable systems
- Marketing: Strong performance with significant improvements in customer acquisition
- Logistics: Faced recurring delays due to supply chain inefficiencies
- Customer Support: Struggled with high ticket volumes and slower response times

Customer Metrics:
Customer acquisition increased significantly during the year due to successful marketing campaigns.
However, customer satisfaction experienced fluctuations due to delays in logistics and support response times.

Operational Highlights:
- Successful expansion into new geographic markets
- Increased investment in infrastructure and cloud services
- Launch of multiple product enhancements

Operational Challenges:
- Supply chain disruptions impacting logistics efficiency
- Rising operational and infrastructure costs
- Increased pressure on customer support teams

Key Risks:
- Supply chain instability affecting delivery timelines
- Rising infrastructure and cloud costs impacting profitability
- Attrition in support teams leading to reduced service quality

Strategy and Initiatives:
- Optimize infrastructure to control costs
- Strengthen logistics through improved vendor partnerships
- Expand customer support teams and improve response systems
- Continue investment in marketing for customer growth

Financial Outlook:
The company expects continued growth in the upcoming year, but at a moderated pace.
Focus will be on improving operational efficiency and cost management.

Conclusion:
Overall, the company performed well in 2025 with strong contributions from Engineering and Marketing.
However, Logistics and Customer Support require focused improvements to address operational inefficiencies and maintain customer satisfaction.
